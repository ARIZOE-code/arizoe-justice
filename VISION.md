# AI Principles

Every public submission will pass through AI verification before publication.

AI will check:

- Fake News
- Duplicate Reports
- Hate Speech
- Abusive Content
- Spam
- Misinformation
- Image Validation
- Basic Fact Consistency

Only verified content will be eligible for publication.
