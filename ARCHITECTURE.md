# ARIZOE JUSTICE SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE

# Overview

ARIZOE JUSTICE is a Digital Justice Platform designed to collect, verify, organize and publish public issues using AI-assisted verification while maintaining transparency, privacy and constitutional values.

---

# User Roles

## Founder

Full system control.

Can manage everything.

---

## Executive Dashboard

Invite only.

Visible to Founder only unless explicitly made public.

Permissions:

- Review Reports
- Manage Content
- Verify Cases
- Manage Volunteers
- View Analytics

---

## Verified Contributors

Can publish articles after AI verification.

Can upload legal resources.

Can assist in campaigns.

---

## Registered Members

- Submit complaints
- Track submissions
- Save documents
- Receive notifications

---

## Public Users

Read-only access.

Can browse news, campaigns and legal resources.

---

# AI Verification Engine

Every submission passes through AI.

Checks:

- Spam
- Duplicate Content
- Fake News
- Image Manipulation
- Hate Speech
- Abusive Language
- Missing Evidence
- Source Reliability
- Basic Fact Consistency

Only content passing required checks becomes eligible for publication.
